### Applicable Regulations

**YouTube Terms of Service - Automated Systems Restrictions**

YouTube's Terms prohibit using automated systems, including "robots," "spiders," or "offline readers," that access the service in a manner that sends more request messages than a human can reasonably produce. For developers, YouTube may monitor, review and inspect API Clients, and monitor and audit access to and use of the YouTube API Services at any time to ensure quality and verify compliance.
